Handmade dough from scratch with freshly shredded mozzarella cheese topped with beef pepperoni


ingredients

  • shredded mozzarella (or cheese of choice)
  • beef pepperoni (or meat of choice)

steps

  2. bring to desied shape
  3. add pizza sauce
